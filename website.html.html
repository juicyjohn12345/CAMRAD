About CAMRAD

UK based and Physician led teleradiology built on clinical excellence and radiologist satisfaction.

CAMRAD Ltd was founded by consultant radiologists with extensive NHS experience in Northwest England. Having worked in busy radiology departments across Cheshire and Mersey, we've experienced firsthand the workforce pressures facing modern radiology services.

We saw an opportunity to create something different: a teleradiology provider that genuinely prioritizes clinical quality and radiologist wellbeing. We pay our reporting radiologists approximately 57% of revenue — significantly above the industry standard. Happy, fairly compensated radiologists produce better reports.
