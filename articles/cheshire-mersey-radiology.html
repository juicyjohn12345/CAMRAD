    CAMRAD was founded in Chester by consultant radiologists who have worked within NHS trusts across Cheshire and Mersey. That regional grounding is not incidental — it shapes everything about how we operate.

    We know your pressures

    Having worked in NHS radiology departments across the region, our founders understand the specific workforce challenges, referral patterns, and operational constraints facing trusts in this area. We are not a distant provider offering a generic service — we are a local team with genuine regional expertise.

    Dedicated account management

    Every CAMRAD trust partner receives dedicated account management. That means a named contact, regular reporting on turnaround and quality metrics, and rapid escalation pathways if issues arise. We are accessible in a way that national providers often are not.

    Technical integration

    We support PACS connectivity via secure VPN, RIS integration, and HL7 messaging. Our technical team works alongside your IT department to ensure smooth onboarding with minimal disruption to existing workflows.
