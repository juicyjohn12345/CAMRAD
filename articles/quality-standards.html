    When an NHS trust commissions a radiology report, clinical quality cannot be compromised for the sake of speed or cost. At CAMRAD, we have built our entire service model around ensuring that every report meets the standards you would expect from your own in-house consultant team.

    GMC-registered consultants only

    Every radiologist reporting for CAMRAD holds full GMC registration and has completed specialist training in the UK. We do not use overseas-trained radiologists reporting outside of a UK regulatory framework. This is a non-negotiable baseline for us.

    Structured reporting

    All reports follow structured templates aligned with Royal College of Radiologists (RCR) guidelines. Structured reporting reduces omission errors, improves clarity for the referring clinician, and facilitates audit.

    Peer review programme

    We operate a 5% random peer review programme across all reporting output. Reports are reviewed by a second consultant radiologist against a standardised scoring framework. Results are shared with individual radiologists as part of ongoing professional development.

    Our compliance framework

    • ISO27001-aligned information security management
    • GDPR compliant with signed Data Processing Agreements
    • NHS Digital Data Security & Protection Toolkit
    • Cyber Essentials Certified (Q2 2026)
    • CQC Registration in progress (Q3 2026)
    • Professional Indemnity Insurance: £10M
